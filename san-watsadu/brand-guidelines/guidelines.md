# San Watsadu (สรรวัสดุ) Brand Guidelines

**Tagline**: สรร ทดสอบ ไว้ใจได้ (Curated. Tested. Trusted.)

# General Guidelines

* Apply San Watsadu's official brand identity consistently across all designs
* Maintain warmth and approachability while staying professional
* Prioritize clarity over complexity in every design choice
* Use white space generously—it creates calm and focus
* Ensure high contrast and readable fonts for accessibility

# Design System Guidelines

## Colors

### Primary Colors
* Forest Green `#2C5F2D` - Use for primary brand color, headers, calls-to-action
* Warm Clay `#D4A574` - Use for accents, highlights, warmth in designs

### Secondary Colors
* Steel Gray `#6B7280` - Use for supporting text, borders, neutral backgrounds
* Clean White `#FFFFFF` - Use for backgrounds, breathing room, digital interfaces
* Deep Charcoal `#1F2937` - Use for primary text, strong statements, premium products

### Accent/Utility Colors
* Alert Red `#DC2626` - Use for warnings, urgent actions
* Success Green `#10B981` - Use for confirmations, positive actions
* Info Blue `#3B82F6` - Use for information, links, interactive elements

## Typography

### Heading Font
* **Prompt (Thai) + Inter** - Bold, modern, designed for Thai language
* Use for headers, titles, emphasis
* Strong visual hierarchy, friendly yet professional

### Body Font
* **Noto Sans Thai + Noto Sans** - Clean, modern, excellent Thai language support
* Use for body text, general content, website, apps
* Professional, highly readable, works across digital and print

## Color Application Rules

* Primary actions and headers use Forest Green (`#2C5F2D`)
* Accents and highlights use Warm Clay (`#D4A574`)
* Text uses Deep Charcoal (`#1F2937`) on light backgrounds
* Maintain visual warmth while staying professional
* Smart color selection based on background contrast

## Text Styling Rules

* Headings: Prompt Bold
* Body text: Noto Sans Thai
* Preserve text hierarchy and formatting
* Automatically handle Thai language rendering

# Design Principles

* **Clarity Over Complexity:** Every design choice should serve the user's understanding
* **Breathing Room:** White space is not wasted space—it creates calm and focus
* **Consistent Visual Hierarchy:** Users should immediately know what's important
* **Warm Technology:** Digital doesn't mean cold. Bring humanity into every interface
* **Accessible for All:** High contrast, readable fonts, clear labels for everyone
